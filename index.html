Farm Day

Farm Day is an educational video game for the company Legend Of Learning. The game is about solving mini-games and puzzles with the aim of learning concepts about chemistry and physics.

The project was developed entirely with Unity, the scripts are programmed in C# and json files were used to manage the loading of different languages.

AI Space Explorer

AI Space Explorer is an educational video game for Legend Of Learning. The game consists of solving mini-games and puzzles with the aim of learning concepts about the solar system.

The project was developed with Unity.

Geological Park

Geological Park is an educational 3D video game for Legend Of Learning. In this game you will have to explore the park to recover a collection of stones, learning about the stone cycle.

The project was developed with Unity.

The Ball Matter

The Ball Matter is an educational video game for Legend Of Learning. In The Ball Matter you must use a simulator to see and learn about the properties of matter.

The project was developed with Unity.

Plants Vs. CO2

Plants Vs. CO2 is an educational video game for Legend Of Learning. In Plants Vs. CO2 you must form an army of plants to carry out photosynthesis and eliminate carbon dioxide from a planet with a lot of pollution.

The project was developed with Unity.

Tiny's Journey

Tiny's Journey is an educational video game for Legend Of Learning. In Tiny's Journey you will travel through different biomes with different living and non-living beings to learn about them.

The project was developed with Unity.

Space Adventures

Space Adventures is an educational video game for Legend Of Learning. In Space Adventures you start a journey on the space station to learn about the Earth and the solar system.

The project was developed with Unity.

Space Academy

Space Academy is an educational video game for Legend Of Learning. At Space Academy you must pass the courses on the universe and its stars to become an astronaut.

The project was developed with Unity.

Rise of ancient Egipt

The project was developed with Unity.
